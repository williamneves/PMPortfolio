About Me

Farbod Jahan is a Product Manager and Technologist living in San Francisco, California. He currently works as a Sr. Technical Product Manager at Paypal. Farbod is an advocate and defender of technology, design, and security.

Farbod received his MBA from Santa Clara University and graduated from San Jose State University, where he studied computer engineering for his Bachelors of Science. Since 2012 he has worked at enterprise-scale, hyper-growth technology companies including: Spell Train, Guidewire Software, ScreenMeet, Paypal .

When not working, Farbod can be found researching the latest technologies and trends, working out, or enjoying the great San Francisco sites. He also enjoys playing the piano and mentoring young technologists in the Bay Area looking to start careers in the tech.
