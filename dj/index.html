My passion for electronic music started over 10 years ago when I got my first taste of what electronic music was. Over the years my passion for this music has grown to the point where I started to explore Djing as a hobby. When the world was lockedown and I could no longer see my favorite artists live I took matters into my own hands and tried to recreate this experience from my home.

I picked up my first controller back in 2020 and had been practicing every week. Trying different genres and styles till I found what my sound is. Every show I put all my passion into the decks and try to recreate the dance floor as I would enjoy listening to my favorite artist. Tune into the livestream to experience what DJ FJ has to offer.

Let’s make summer feel like it lasts all year with a soundtrack of the best summer hits
